I am Gabriele Pongelli, a university student who has decided to create a blog in which to share his projects and his progresses. I recently started to approach the world of cybersecurity and to try to complete some challenges focused on it, so I will post here also the solutions of some of them.

My Path

$ cat ~/.bash_history

I attended the A. Malignani Technical Institute in Udine and I graduated in computer science and telecommunications. After that I enrolled at the University of Udine for the three-year degree course in computer science, where I am now. I participated in the CyberChallenge program in the 2019/2020 edition and thanks to this I started taking an interest in cybersecurity. I am part of the ethical hackers team of the University of Udine: the MadrHacks, with whom I participated in a few Capture the Flag events of ctftime.
